About the GAD Database System

The Gender and Development (GAD) Database System of the Municipality of Gabaldon is designed to organize, manage, and provide easy access to Gender and Development information. It serves as a centralized repository of GAD Plans and Budgets, Accomplishment Reports, statistical data, and other relevant documents that support gender-responsive governance.

Vision

To become a reliable and accessible digital information system that promotes gender equality, inclusive governance, and evidence-based planning in the Municipality of Gabaldon.

Mission

To strengthen Gender and Development initiatives by providing accurate, organized, and accessible information that supports planning, implementation, monitoring, and evaluation of GAD programs and activities.

Objectives

• Centralize GAD records and documents.
• Improve access to GAD Plans and Budgets.
• Support monitoring and reporting.
• Promote transparency and accountability.
• Strengthen gender-responsive governance.

GAD Focal Point System (GFPS)

The GAD Focal Point System (GFPS) serves as the official mechanism responsible for planning, implementing, monitoring, and evaluating Gender and Development (GAD) programs, projects, and activities of the Municipality of Gabaldon.
